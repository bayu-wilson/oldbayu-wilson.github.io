Create-Your-Own-Constellation Astronomy Activity

This purpose of this activity is to provide elementary-aged youth with a broader perspective of the night sky. The night sky is scattered with stars that can be grouped into constellations. Throughout human history, people imagined constellations in the night sky to share their myths & stories, guide them through the night, and keep track of time. This activity highlights the expanse of cultural and physical perspectives of the night sky.
